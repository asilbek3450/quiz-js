# Privacy Policy

## Play Market uchun Privacy Policy URL

**URL:** `https://jahonschool.pythonanywhere.com/privacy`

Bu sahifa veb-sayt va mobil ilova uchun yagona Privacy Policy hisoblanadi.
Google Play Console'da yuqoridagi URL ni "Privacy policy" maydoniga kiriting.

## Data Safety Form uchun javoblar

Play Console "Data safety" formasida quyidagilarni belgilang:

- **Data collected**: Ism, sinf, test natijalari (User Content)
- **Data shared**: Hech qanday ma'lumot uchinchi tomonga uzatilmaydi
- **Encryption**: Ha (HTTPS orqali)
- **Data deletion**: Foydalanuvchi so'rovi orqali o'chirilishi mumkin
- **Permissions**: Faqat `INTERNET`
